Tip of the week

Stay away from speculation, technology, and commodities.

Speculation is defined as any investment that you hope to gain out of, but risks a lot, such as penny stocks. Do not invest in technology either, since it is very risky. However, If the company gives up to five percent dividend, such as Apple, you could invest in it. Finally, commodities are things such as gold, silver, and oil, which depend on scarcity, and sometimes luck, which makes them very much like a speculation.
